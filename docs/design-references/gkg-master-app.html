Objection Handling Playbook
"We already have SAP"
Response: Aerchain isn't here to replace SAP — it's here to make your SAP investment smarter. We sit on top of SAP (or Oracle, ServiceNow) and add the AI intelligence layer that ERP systems were never designed to provide. Think of us as the autonomous procurement brain that leverages your existing ERP data. Our integration takes 2 weeks, not 12 months.
"Too expensive"
Response: Our customers typically see 12-18% procurement cost savings within 6 months. On a $100M annual spend, that's $12-18M in savings against a platform cost of $200-500K. The ROI isn't 2x or 5x — it's 30-60x. We're happy to build a custom ROI model for your spend profile.
"We're not ready for AI"
Response: That's exactly why you need Aerchain now. Our platform meets you where you are — even if your procurement is running on spreadsheets today, our AI learns from your data on day one. The longer you wait, the wider the gap becomes between your procurement maturity and your competitors'. ADNOC wasn't "AI-ready" either — but 90 days after deployment, they had full autonomous sourcing running.
"We need to see ROI first"
Response: Completely fair. That's why we offer a 60-day POC with a defined success criteria framework. We'll pick 2-3 categories, run autonomous sourcing against your current process, and measure: cycle time reduction, cost savings, and compliance improvements. If we don't hit the agreed benchmarks, you walk away with zero cost.
"Our procurement team won't adopt it"
Response: Legacy tools fail on adoption because they add work. Aerchain removes work. Our platform automates the 70% of procurement tasks that are repetitive — your team gets to focus on strategic decisions, supplier relationships, and negotiations. At Unilever, user adoption hit 85% in the first month because procurement teams loved not doing manual RFx processing anymore.
"What about data security?"
Response: SOC2 Type II certified, ISO 27001 compliant, GDPR-ready. All data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We support SSO via SAML/OIDC, role-based access control, and full audit logging. We've passed security assessments from ADNOC, EGA, and Iron Mountain — some of the most security-conscious enterprises globally.
"Can you integrate with our existing systems?"
Response: API-first architecture means we integrate with anything. Pre-built connectors for SAP, Oracle, ServiceNow, Salesforce, Microsoft D365, and 50+ other platforms. Custom integrations via REST API typically take 1-2 weeks. We have a dedicated integration engineering team.
"You're too small / startup risk"
Response: $13M Series A from top-tier VCs, 95 employees, customers across 15+ countries including Fortune 500 names. We're small enough to give you white-glove attention and big enough to deliver enterprise-grade reliability. Uptime: 99.95% SLA. Our smaller size means your feedback shapes the product roadmap — something you'll never get from Coupa or SAP.
